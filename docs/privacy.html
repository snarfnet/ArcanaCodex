Arcana Library - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-19

Overview

Arcana Library is a tarot and symbolism reference app. The app stores its learning content on your device and does not require you to create an account.

Advertising

Arcana Library uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, approximate location, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and other data needed to deliver, measure, and improve ads.

Google may use this information according to its own policies. Please see Google's privacy policy for details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

Arcana Library does not include a separate analytics service. AdMob may provide advertising-related measurement and diagnostics.

Personal Information

Arcana Library does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or account information.

Network Access

The app may access the network to load advertisements from Google AdMob.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at snarfnet@gmail.com.
